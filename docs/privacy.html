Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

Minute Zero Auto-Post ("the App") is an automated content publishing tool that posts videos to TikTok on behalf of the authorized account owner.

Data We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share personal data from any users other than the single TikTok account that has authorized the App. No user data is transmitted to third parties.

TikTok API Usage

The App uses the TikTok Content Posting API solely to upload video content to the authorized account (@minutezero3). Access tokens are stored locally and used only for this purpose.
