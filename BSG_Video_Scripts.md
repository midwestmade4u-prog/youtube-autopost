# Bible Story Garden — 10 Video Scripts
### Ready to paste into the Video Studio app

**How to use:**
1. Open the app at localhost:5002
2. Scroll down to Scenes → click **📋 Import Script from Claude**
3. Copy the JSON block for the video you want
4. Paste it in and click **Load Script**
5. Hit **Generate Video**

---

## Why These 10?
Based on YouTube performance data for family/Bible channels:
- **David & Goliath, Noah, Moses** — highest search volume for kids Bible stories
- **Daniel, Jonah, Zacchaeus** — strong engagement, great visuals, clear moral
- **Jeremiah 29:11, John 3:16, Philippians 4:13** — most shared Bible verses by parents
- **Birth of Jesus** — evergreen + seasonal spike every Christmas

> ✅ You already have: Noah's Ark, Psalm 23, David and Goliath
